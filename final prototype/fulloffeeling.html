FULL OF FEELING (2017)

Original Song and Music Video
Stylist: Joe Traptow, Videographer: Angus McMaster, Couture: Xavier LaRuelle
Music Video premiered through Vice

Full of feeling is a narrative journey into the everlasting ennui of the future's immortal woman and her driver. In her world the saturation of experience is universal, leaving nothing remaining except the bleeding of colours and acceleration towards eternity.

All costumes were designed for this video in creative collaboration with Montreal-based couturier Xavier LaRuelle and creative director Joel Traptow.
