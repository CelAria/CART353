RESOLUTION (2017)

Audio/Visual Installation
In collaboration with Eric Morgan
Installed at Le Livart for Nuit Blanche 2017

Resolution, an interactive media installation, explores the parallels between the disintegration of memories and data corruption in the digital world. Using a multi-coloured led candle as a controller, the viewer can draw in space to erode photo-memories belonging to the artist, jointly creating a responsive ambient sonic experience.

What algorithmic tendencies belong to the human memory? If given root control can we force our logic systems to corrupt and recontextualize our experiences?
