TRACES: FLEURESCENT (2017)

Audio/Visual Installation
In collaboration with Sherman Fong, Dimitri Kontogiannos, Charlotte Rothsching
Project Website

Traces is an interactive artwork that addresses the urgent issues of ecological contamination and data dissemination.

A biological entity, a flower, becomes a way of graphing human digital interaction and thought, subsuming the data provided by viewers via Twitter. The artwork provides a new mode of visualizing and interacting with political-social-cultural information through an eco-digital reference point.

The relationship between big data and the ecological, often posed in opposition to each other- are indivisibly tied together in a dependant relationship.

The reaction of the flower to the data conditions creates the dialogue between the biological and digital information systems. The end product is the bioaccumulation of toxicants fueled by human interaction with technology.
